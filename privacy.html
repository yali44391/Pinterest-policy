Pinterest Addon Privacy Policy ​we respect your privacy. This policy outlines how data is handled when using the Pinterest integration feature within our chat application. ​1. Data Collection We do not collect or store personal user data through the Pinterest addon. The only data processed is the "search query" you enter to retrieve images. ​2. Third-Party Services This feature utilizes the Pinterest API. By using this feature, you acknowledge and agree to Pinterest's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. ​Search queries are sent directly to Pinterest servers to fetch results. ​We do not share your user account details or private conversation logs with Pinterest. ​3. Content Usage Image links selected and sent within the chat are stored in our chat logs solely for display purposes. We do not claim ownership of any content retrieved via Pinterest.
